Make a basketball styled mycareer game in python called ROWELEAGUE

This league will have 20-30 teams with their own names
each team will start with 12 players that have their own randomized names and age

Once the player starts the game they will be prompted to enter a first and last name, height, position, weight, jersey number, and choose a player archetype.

Games will be decided based on overall, the higher a team's overall the better chance they will have at winning a game.

Playoffs will be based off nba's playoff system without the inclusion of the play-in tournament.

After each win and loss the player will recieve coins that can be used to upgrade their team.
this will encourage the player to stay with their drafted team instead of requesting a trade or leaving in free agency.

teams will be able to cut and trade players based off their needs.

Players who are ages 37-39 have a chance to retire, once a player reaches age 40 they are gauranteed to retire.

players will improve yearly based off their age, ages 26-31 are the prime years of a player, after those years the players will begin to regress due to age. 

The player's objective is to try to make the hall of fame, they will do this by accumulating LEGEND POINTS by winning league awards and championships.
